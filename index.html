About Us

University Travel is a locally owned family business, we offer distinctive, itineraries with unparalleled, personalized service. Our passion has never changed, creating adventures and memories for any budget. We specialize in European and Caribbean leisure travel. We work hard to keep our clients happy; and are proud to have a high percentage of repeat business and referrals, Owner, Petra, is the consummate world traveler; having traveled all over the world and has valuable expertise in all aspects of travel whether it's leisure, business or group travel. In our aim to provide the best service, we offer you unbiased information on your best air, car, hotel, rail and cruise options. Our preferred relationships will be available to you with value- added amenities and supplier discounts.

Our Founder

U-TRAV in Acton, Massachusetts was founded in July 1984 by Petra Garbero. Petra learned every aspect of the travel business from her parents who were international travel agents. Petra has traveled extensively over the years and has valuable expertise in all aspects of travel whether it's leisure, business or group travel.

Our Services

University Travel will make your travel easy, by managing all the details and coordinating a customized itinerary. Coordinating air, ground transportations, hotels and sightseeing to the destination of your choice. Let us share our knowledge with you to make your next vacation, destination wedding, honeymoon or event, your most memorable. Call us today!
