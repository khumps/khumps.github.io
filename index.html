Who We Are

University Travel is a family business, our philosophy has been the same for over 25 years, offering distinctive, itineraries with unparalleled, personalized service. Our passion has never changed, creating adventures and memories for any budget. We specialize in European and Caribbean leisure travel. Owner, Petra, is the consummate world traveler; having traveled all over the world and has valuable expertise in all aspects of travel whether it's leisure, business or group travel.
"The world is full of amazing moments to feel, taste, smell, hear and see, and we can connect your to all of them! "

Our Founder

U-TRAV in Acton, Massachusetts was founded in July 1984 by Petra Garbero. Petra learned every aspect of the travel business from her parents who were international travel agents. Petra has traveled extensively over the years and has valuable expertise in all aspects of travel whether it's leisure, business or group travel.

Our Services

Our vacation planning services will take care of your getaways and coordinate a customized itinerary. Services also include cruise, air, car, hotel, rail, transfers and sightseeing to the destination of your choice. Every element of your trip is reviewed to maximize your discounts. Let us share our knowledge with you to make your next vacation, destination wedding, honeymoon or event, your most memorable. Call us today!
