Our History

For the past 20 years U-TRAV has held to just one commitment, and that is to provide you, our clients, with the best travel service possible. We care about the service we give you. We care about saving you time. We care about saving you money. At U-TRAV you'll always get highly personalized attention from our experienced, professional travel specialists. With our extensive experience, knowledge about travel suppliers, volume discount arrangements and negotiating skills in the travel industry, you'll always get the lowest possible rates. Make us your number one choice for your leisure or business travel.

Our Founder

Petra Garbero, Founder and President U-TRAV in Acton, Massachusetts was founded in July 1984 by Petra Garbero. Petra learned every aspect of the travel business from her parents who were international travel agents. Petra has traveled extensively over the years and has valuable expertise in all aspects of travel whether it's leisure, business or group travel.
