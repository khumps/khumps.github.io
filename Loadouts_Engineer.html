Role The Engineer is one of the two support class and can play a very important role when he/she does their job correctly. The engineer has a few main jobs.
  1. Provide ammo
  2. Repair anything that needs repairing including but not limited to
    • Turrets
    • Maxes
    • Vehicles
  3. Place turrets as shields for friendlies
  4. Use their turrets to supress enemy infintry and tanks
Loadout There are several loadouts that an engineer can run that are all very good. But there are some key things the engineer must get.

The first thing the engineer should really strive to get is to max out the levels on their repair tool and ammo pack if you plan on being primarily an engineer. But even if you dont plan on that you should at least max out them anyway.

The next things you should get are new guns. The two guns I primarily use are the Serpent VE92 and the Solstice SF(select fire). Each one of these has a key role and a different purpose. The Serpent is a good close range weapon. Its high rate of fire makes it very good for close engagements and easier to comeback from slow reflexes(not being the first one to shoot)
