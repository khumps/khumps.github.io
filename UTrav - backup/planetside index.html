Strategy Guide

Planetside 2 is a game of strategy and coordination. If you wish to capture points and hold territories you must work together with your faction.

If you are looking to become a better player of Planetside 2, whether you are looking for strategies or just good choices for loadouts, you have come to the right place.

All strategies on this site are assuming you have a full squad.

Warning

These Strategies are NOT guaranteed to work I take no responsibility if you fail miserably. Also I will be doing all of these as Vanu Soverignty. However these will work with the other factions equivalent weapons.
