Breaching

To breach the powerhouse you will need to get past the recovery floor. This is where most people will make their stand do to its higher position and good choke points. To get past these choke points you will need Explosives

What you will need
  • Classes
    • 2 Medics
    • 2 Engineers
    • 2 MAX
    • 1 Infiltrator
    • 4 Heavy Assaults
    • 1 Light Assaults
Procedure
  1. Engineer: Place ammo outside the back door for the infiltrator.
  2. Infiltrator: Spam darts or place a motion spotter.
  3. At the bottom of the stairs out of view of the enemy form up in order of Max, Heavy Assaults and Engineers, Medics with MAX being closest to the stairs.
  4. Heavy Assaults: Throw Concussion Grenades from bottom of stairs up to the top level
  5. As Soon as Concussion Grenades go off, All people in bottom floor rush up
  6. For maximum effect you can do this at both staircases as there are two
  7. Medics can stay back a bit and throw revive grenades as needed.
Defending If done strategicly, this base is very easy to defend up to very high odds. With one squad we were able to hold it against 48+ (30%VS against 70%NC). When you are defending remember Choke Points Are Key. The only way they can get in to the top are from 2 Staircases on the bottom, windows on the second floor, and 2 roof staircases. The windows and roof staircases will only be accessible by light assaults which means most of your forces can be dedicated to defending the two ground staircases. What You Will Need
  • Classes
    • 2 Medics
    • 3 Engineers
    • 1 Infiltrator
    • 4 Heavy Assaults
    • 2 Maxes
Procedure
  • Divide the squad up for each room. In each room there should be
    • 1 Max
    • 1 Engineer
    • 1 Medic
    • 2 Heavy Assaults
  • The final engineer should help on any side that is getting hit hard.
  • The Infiltrator will work from the center
    1. Engineers: Place your ammo packs in the center of your respective rooms. Also place your turrets as shields at the top of the staircases.
    2. Third Engineer: Place your ammo pack in the center doorway for the infiltrator.
    3. Infiltrator: Start spamming darts or place motion spotter
    4. Medics: Stand out of site of any windows or other entrances where you could be shot from.
    5. Maxes and Heavy Assaults: Position at the top of your respective staircases
    6. Engineers: Place your C-4 on the staircase overhangs.
